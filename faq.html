What time is check-in/ check-out?

Check-in: 4pm

Check-out: 12pm

Early check-in and late check-out is based on availability for an additional fee

Is there a coffee maker in the room?

Complimentary coffee makers are available based upon request.

Is there a refrigerator in the room?

Refrigerators are available upon request for a minimal fee.

Does the hotel have parking?

The hotel does not have a parking garage however valet and self- parking options are available. Valet parking is located in front of the hotel where one of the door men will be happy to assist you with your vehicle.

CON Parking is located at 1 Penn Plaza (34th street between 7th and 8th avenue). Rates are calculated per 24 hours, with no in and out privileges. On the day of departure the vehicle must be retrieved at the exact time it was dropped off (or earlier) to avoid incurring additional charges.

Valet Parking is $60.00 for a sedan and $70.00 for an SUV

Self-parking is $40.00 for a sedan and $50.00 for an SUV

Does the hotel have rollaway beds available?

Rollaway beds are available for an additional fee of $25 per night plus taxes. Not all rooms are equipped to fit a rollaway bed. Please contact the hotel in advance should you require an additional bed in the room.

Is the hotel pet friendly?

We welcome 4 legged guests as well. There is a pet cleaning fee of $25 per night for each pet. No more than 2 pets are allowed in the guest room. The pet must be in a crate when the guest is not in the room.

Is Wi-Fi included?

Premium Wi-Fi is included in the daily facility fee

Is there a fitness center?

Our 24-hour Fitness Center is located in the lower lobby. It includes state-of-the-art fitness bikes, treadmills, free weights, and weight machines.
