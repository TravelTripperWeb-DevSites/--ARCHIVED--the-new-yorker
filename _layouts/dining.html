Gastronomy

The restaurant and lounge offerings at The New Yorker Hotel are guaranteed to please even the most discerning palates. Whether you’re looking to satisfy a late-night craving at Tick Tock Diner, indulge in some hearty Tuscan cuisine, or sip a cocktail with friends, we’ve got you covered.

Tick Tock Diner

This 24-hour diner is a New York institution, dishing out delicious American-style comfort food – everything from juicy burgers to crisp, fresh salads and an all-day breakfast menu. We’re particularly famous for our sandwiches – treat yourself to a Monte Christo, or a Triple Decker Club.
